Mouth of Sauron

Shut the short-form feed.

A Chrome extension that blocks Shorts and Reels on YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok — until you decide to open the maw.

⬇ Download & install View on GitHub

"The Mouth of Sauron was the herald that spoke the endless words of the Dark Tower." This shuts it.

What it blocks

PlatformBehavior when warding is on (default)
YouTube Hides Shorts shelves, sidebar & mini-guide Shorts entries, and Shorts grid rows. Redirects /shorts/<id> to the normal watch page.
Facebook Hides Reels feed cards and the Reels nav entry. Redirects /reel/… to the home feed.
Instagram Hides the Reels nav entry. Redirects /reels/… to home.
TikTok The whole site is short-form, so it's held behind a block curtain. Toggle TikTok off to enter.
Blocking is on by default. The toolbar popup gives you a master switch plus one toggle per site — the toggle is the "unless turned off." Settings sync across your Chrome profile.

Install (unpacked)

  1. Download the extension and unzip it somewhere permanent.
  2. Open chrome://extensions in Chrome (or any Chromium browser).
  3. Turn on Developer mode (top-right toggle).
  4. Click Load unpacked and select the unzipped folder.
  5. Pin the fanged icon to your toolbar. Click it to toggle sites.
